About Wake Up Africa Uganda

Wake Up Africa-Uganda (WAKU) is a refugee-led community group in Zone 3, Bidibidi. From January 2024 to June 2025 WAKU implemented inclusive programs in education, health, livelihoods, youth empowerment and peacebuilding reaching over 1,800 direct beneficiaries.

  • Focus Areas: Education, Health, Livelihoods, Environment, Youth & Peacebuilding
  • Status: Pre-transition to RLO / CBO
  • Goal: Strengthen community-led services and local leadership
